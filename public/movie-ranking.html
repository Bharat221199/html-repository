The Best Movies According to Bharat H

My top 3 movies of all-time

True Lies

I love this movie. It's a James Bond send-up that's Cameron getting into a sentimental phase in his career.

Titanic

Cameron used the Titanic and its tragic sinking as the canvas for his most epic, ambitious filmmaking up to that point.

Avatar

Cameron broke the box office record of his own film, Titanic, when he came up with the sci-fi blockbuster, Avatar, in 2009.
